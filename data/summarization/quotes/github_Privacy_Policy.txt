"[do not track](https://www.eff.org/issues/do-not-track)" (dnt) is a privacy
preference you can set in your browser if you do not want online services to
collect and share certain kinds of information about your online activity from
third party tracking services. [CLS] [SEP] github responds to browser dnt signals and
follows the [w3c standard for responding to dnt signals](https://www.w3.org/tr/tracking-dnt/). [CLS] [SEP] if you have not enabled dnt on a browser that supports it, cookies on some parts of our website will track your online browsing activity on other online services over time, though we do not permit third parties other than our analytics and service providers to track github users' activity over time on github. [CLS] [SEP] we do not sell your personal information and we do
not host advertising on github. [CLS] [SEP] you may also track changes in our site policy repository. [CLS] [SEP] if you are a child under the age of 13, you may not have an account on github. [CLS] [SEP]
