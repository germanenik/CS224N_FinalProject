#  group video calling fair usage policy

group video calls are subject to a fair usage limit of 100 hours per month
with no more than 10 hours per day and a limit of 4 hours per individual video
call. [CLS] [SEP] once these limits have been reached, the video will switch off and the
call will convert to an audio call. [CLS] [SEP]
