**updated** \- 12/26/2019

  * ### privacy policy updates

thank you for reviewing fur affinity's privacy policy for the fur affinity
website located at furaffinity.net and subdomains thereof ("site"). [CLS] [SEP] this
privacy policy details certain policies implemented throughout fur affinity
governing fur affinity's collection and use of personally identifiable
information about users of our site. [CLS] [SEP]

## privacy policy updates

fur affinity may need to update this privacy policy from time to time. [CLS] [SEP] if so,
fur affinity will post our updated privacy policy on our site, along with
notice that the privacy policy has been changed so you are always aware of
what personally identifiable information we may collect and how we may use
this information. [CLS] [SEP] fur affinity may also send users a notification notifying
such users of any changes to the privacy policy. [CLS] [SEP] fur affinity encourages you
to review this privacy policy regularly for any changes. [CLS] [SEP] your continued use of
the site and/or continued provision of personally identifiable information to
us will be subject to the terms of the then-current privacy policy. [CLS] [SEP]

## information collection and use

to register an account at fur affinity we request certain personal data from
you on the registration form such as your username, password, email address,
and date of birth. [CLS] [SEP]

we also collect the location data, including city, metro code, zip code,
state, country, latitude and longitude and area code through your ip address
as well as your browser id. [CLS] [SEP]

in addition, when you register, we may collect any personal data that you
personally elect to include in your public profile, including email addresses
or contact information. [CLS] [SEP]

unless you have set your preferences to hide certain data other users on our
service will be able to view your profile data (which includes your username
or any customizable data which you may have provided, such as a bio, contact
information, or personal preferences). [CLS] [SEP]

## children's privacy

fur affinity recognizes the privacy interests of children and we encourage
parents and guardians to take an active role in their children's online
activities and interests. [CLS] [SEP] the site is not directed to children under the age
of 13. [CLS] [SEP] fur affinity does not target its site to children under 13. [CLS] [SEP] fur
affinity does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from
children under the age of 13. [CLS] [SEP]

if we learn that a child under the age of 13 provided us with personally
identifiable information, we will delete that information. [CLS] [SEP] if your child has
provided personally identifiable information, please contact us. [CLS] [SEP] if you are
under the age of 13, please do not provide us with any personally identifiable
information. [CLS] [SEP]

please note: for users in the eu the minimum age to create an account is 16. [CLS] [SEP]

## cookies and tracking

we may use small text files called cookies to improve overall experience in
the site. [CLS] [SEP] a cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive
containing information about the user. [CLS] [SEP] we may use persistent cookies to
remember your login to the site. [CLS] [SEP] you can read more about cookies at
www.allaboutcookies.org. [CLS] [SEP] if you reject cookies, you may still use our site,
but your ability to use some areas of our site may be limited. [CLS] [SEP]

fur affinity tracks users over time and over our furaffinity subdomain
websites. [CLS] [SEP] we record users' use of the site, comments they make and the pages
that they visit. [CLS] [SEP] third party providers of external services that provide
advertising, such as doubleclick by google, may track users over time and over
our site and third party apps or websites and such third parties may also
place cookies, via our site. [CLS] [SEP] you can read more about privacy and cookies set
by doubleclick by google at https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies. [CLS] [SEP]

## aggregate information

we may track information that will be maintained, used and disclosed in
aggregate form only and which will not contain your personally identifiable
information. [CLS] [SEP] for example, we may collect ip addresses, browser type and cookie
numbers. [CLS] [SEP] we may analyze this data for trends and statistics in the aggregate,
and we may use such aggregate information to administer the site, track users'
movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. [CLS] [SEP]

## disclosure

we may provide your personally identifiable information and the data generated
by cookies and the aggregate information to the vendors and service agencies
that we may engage to assist us in providing our services to you for their use
solely to provide us with such assistance. [CLS] [SEP] we will also disclose your
personally identifiable information (a) if we are required to do so by law,
regulation or other government authority or otherwise in cooperation with an
ongoing investigation of a governmental authority, (b) to enforce the fur
affinity terms of service or user agreements or to protect our rights or (c)
to protect the safety of users of our site and our services. [CLS] [SEP] we will not sell
your personally identifiable information to any company or organization, but
we may transfer your personally identifiable information to a successor entity
upon a merger, consolidation or other corporate reorganization in which fur
affinity participates or to a purchaser or acquirer of all or substantially
all of fur affinity's assets to which the site relates. [CLS] [SEP]

## california privacy rights

we do not disclose any personally identifiable information to third parties
for their direct marketing purposes. [CLS] [SEP] should this practice change, we will seek
your consent before disclosing information for a third party's direct
marketing. [CLS] [SEP]

california residents wishing to exercise access, data portability, or deletion
rights above may submit a verifiable consumer request to us by either:

  * sending us an email to [privacy@furaffinity.net](mailto:privacy@furaffinity.net). [CLS] [SEP]

## verification and response

we endeavor to respond to a verifiable requests within forty-five (45) days of
receipt. [CLS] [SEP] if we require more time (up to 90 days), we will inform you of the
reason and extension period in writing. [CLS] [SEP] if you have an account with us, we
will deliver our written response to that account. [CLS] [SEP] if you do not have an
account with us, we will deliver our written response by mail or
electronically, at your option. [CLS] [SEP]

any disclosures we provide will only cover the 12-month period preceding our
receipt of the verifiable request. [CLS] [SEP] if applicable, our response will explain
the reasons we cannot comply with a request. [CLS] [SEP] for data portability requests, we
will select a format to provide your personal information that is readily
useable and should allow you to transmit the information from one entity to
another entity without hindrance. [CLS] [SEP]

## links to third party sites

the site may provide links to other websites or resources over which fur
affinity does not have control ("external web sites"). [CLS] [SEP] such links do not
constitute an endorsement by fur affinity of those external web sites. [CLS] [SEP] you
acknowledge that fur affinity is providing these links to you only as a
convenience, and further agree that fur affinity is not responsible for the
content of such external web sites. [CLS] [SEP] your use of external web sites is subject
to the terms of use and privacy policies located on the linked to external web
sites. [CLS] [SEP]

## security

we may employ procedural and technological measures, consistent with industry
practice. [CLS] [SEP] such measures are reasonably designed to help protect your
personally identifiable information from loss, unauthorized access,
disclosure, alteration or destruction. [CLS] [SEP] fur affinity may use encryption, secure
socket layer, password protection and other internal measures to help prevent
unauthorized access to your personally identifiable information. [CLS] [SEP] we also have
internal policies for the safe handling of data. [CLS] [SEP] we will endeavor to destroy
your information when we no longer have a legitimate business need to use it,
except for backups that are archived and retained in the ordinary course of
business. [CLS] [SEP]

## staying in control of your information: your rights

we respect the fact that your personal information is your information, and
we'll make it easy for you to manage and update your personal details. [CLS] [SEP]

### your right to rectification

you have a right to rectify, update or complete any inaccurate personal data,
pending verification of your identification. [CLS] [SEP] most of this can be accomplished
by going to your "account settings" and making any changes there. [CLS] [SEP] for
instance, if your contact details change, or if you spot any errors in the
information we hold about you, we recommend starting with your "account
settings". [CLS] [SEP] if, after visiting your "account settings", you are still unable to
rectify the information, please contact us directly by opening a trouble
ticket. [CLS] [SEP]

requests for your rectification can be sent to
[myinfo@furaffinity.net](mailto:myinfo@furaffinity.net). [CLS] [SEP]

### your right of access

you have the right to obtain a copy of all personal data that fur affinity
holds about you, such as personal details or correspondence, generally, within
a 30 day time limit and free of charge. [CLS] [SEP] you may limit the amount of data that
you would like to obtain if you do not wish to access all of the records. [CLS] [SEP]

requests for your right of access can be sent to
[myinfo@furaffinity.net](mailto:myinfo@furaffinity.net). [CLS] [SEP]

### your right to move your data (portability)

under certain circumstances you can request that a data collector move your
data to another service provider. [CLS] [SEP] this is not an automatic right, and because
fur affinity provides unique services, it may not be able to honor your right
to data portability. [CLS] [SEP]

requests for your right of access can be sent to
[myinfo@furaffinity.net](mailto:myinfo@furaffinity.net). [CLS] [SEP]

### your right to object to processing

you have to right to request that fur affinity stop certain data processing
activities that involve processing your personal data. [CLS] [SEP] this isn't an automatic
right, and fur affinity's ability to restrict processing will depend on the
type of data that for which you are making this request. [CLS] [SEP]

requests for your personal information can be sent to
[stop@furaffinity.net](mailto:stop@furaffinity.net). [CLS] [SEP]

### you have a right request deletion

you have a right to request that fur affinity delete your personal data that
it holds. [CLS] [SEP] this isn't an automatic right, and what fur affinity is able to
delete will depend on the type of data that we hold about you, whether it was
obtained via consent, what purpose it serves, and whether fur affinity has an
obligation to keep the data, among other things. [CLS] [SEP]

note that unless you request deletion, and such request is determined to be
appropriate, while you remain a user, we'll process and retain your data as
described in this privacy notice in order to enable you to use the services. [CLS] [SEP]
once you stop being a user, we will hold your data as described in the data
retention section. [CLS] [SEP]

requests for deletions can be sent to
[deleteme@furaffinity.net](mailto:deleteme@furaffinity.net). [CLS] [SEP]

for all requests mentioned above, there is a 30 day limit to which fur
affinity must respond by, that may be extended in certain situations. [CLS] [SEP] also,
you will need to give adequate proof of your identity in order for fur
affinity to identify you before any data is released. [CLS] [SEP]

### you have a right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

if you feel that fur affinity is not processing your personal data in a manner
that complies with the gdpr, you have a right to lodge a complaint with the
supervisory authority of your country of residence. [CLS] [SEP]

### you have a right to withdraw your consent

for activities where you have provided consent to fur affinity, you have a
right to withdraw your consent at any time by opening a trouble ticket and
notify staff. [CLS] [SEP] note that where you remove consent we may not be able to offer
you continued access to the services. [CLS] [SEP] however, if the consent relates to
activities that are not strictly necessary for a functional user experience,
your withdrawal will not affect your ability to use and access the services. [CLS] [SEP]

## questions

if you have any questions regarding this privacy policy please contact us via
email at [privacy@furaffinity.net](mailto:privacy@furaffinity.net). [CLS] [SEP]

## users outside of the united states

our computer systems and third party hosting provider systems are currently
based in the united states and may be located in other countries, so your
personal data will be processed by us in the u.s. [CLS] [SEP] and other countries where
data protection and privacy regulations may not offer the same level of
protection as in other parts of the world, such as the european union. [CLS] [SEP] if you
create a user account with the site as a visitor from outside the united
states, by using the site you agree to this privacy policy and you consent to
the transfer of all such information to the united states, which may not offer
an equivalent level of protection of that required in the european union or
certain other countries, and to the processing of that information as
described in this privacy policy. [CLS] [SEP]
