# cookie policy

reklamstore (“us”, “we”, or “our”) uses cookies on the www.reklamstore.com
website (the “service”) and its sub-domains. [CLS] [SEP] by using the service, you consent
to the use of cookies. [CLS] [SEP]

our cookies policy explains what cookies are, how we use cookies, how third-
parties we may partner with may use cookies on the service, your choices
regarding cookies and further information about cookies. [CLS] [SEP]

## what are cookies

cookies are small pieces of text sent by your web browser by a website you
visit. [CLS] [SEP] a cookie file is stored in your web browser and allows the service or a
third-party to recognize you and make your next visit easier and the service
more useful to you. [CLS] [SEP]

cookies can be “persistent” or “session” cookies. [CLS] [SEP] persistent cookies remain on
your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while session
cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser. [CLS] [SEP]

## how reklamstore uses cookies on our website

when you use and access the service, we may place a number of cookies files in
your web browser. [CLS] [SEP]  
we use cookies for the following purposes: preferences, statistics, marketing. [CLS] [SEP]

## third-party cookies

in addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-parties cookies
to report usage statistics of the service, deliver advertisements on and
through the service, and so on. [CLS] [SEP]

this site uses google analytics which is one of the most widespread and
trusted analytics solutions on the web for helping us to understand how you
use the site and ways that we can improve your experience. [CLS] [SEP] these cookies may
track things such as how long you spend on the site and the pages that you
visit so we can continue to produce engaging content. [CLS] [SEP]

## what are your choices as our visitor regarding cookies

if you’d like to delete cookies or instruct your web browser to delete or
refuse cookies, please visit the help pages of your web browser. [CLS] [SEP]

please note, however, that if you delete cookies or refuse to accept them, you
might not be able to use all of the features and services reklamstore offers,
you may not be able to store your preferences, and some of our pages might not
display properly. [CLS] [SEP]

  * internet explorer web browser: [how to delete cookie files in internet explorer](http://support.microsoft.com/kb/278835)
  * firefox web browser: [delete cookies to remove the information websites have stored on your computer](https://support.mozilla.org/en-us/kb/delete-cookies-remove-info-websites-stored)
  * safari web browser: [manage cookies and website data using safari](https://support.apple.com/kb/ph21411?locale=en_us)
  * for the chrome web browser: [clear cache & cookies](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/32050)
  * for any other web browser, please visit your web browser’s official web pages. [CLS] [SEP]
