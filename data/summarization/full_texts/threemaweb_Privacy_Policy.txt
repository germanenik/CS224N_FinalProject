# privacy policy

_march 6, 2018_

threema gmbh (hereafter «threema») was founded on the premise of bulletproof
data protection. [CLS] [SEP] it is our primary goal to store only the absolute minimum of
information for the shortest possible time («privacy by design»). [CLS] [SEP] in addition
to using state-of-the-art encryption methods, we take all necessary technical
and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized data access and misuse. [CLS] [SEP]
the processing and protection of data is carried out in accordance with
applicable legal regulations and eu regulation 2016/679 (gdpr). [CLS] [SEP]

#### 1\. [CLS] [SEP] general information

the threema website can be used completely anonymously and is completely free
of advertising, tracking or other analytics software. [CLS] [SEP]

#### 2\. [CLS] [SEP] purpose of data processing

threema only processes data to respond to your support request or to enable
orders in the threema merchandise shop. [CLS] [SEP]

#### 3\. [CLS] [SEP] scope and duration of data processing

  * [requests for technical support](/en/support) contain your email address, which is necessary to answer the request. [CLS] [SEP] support requests and all of the associated data will be permanently deleted after 6 months of inactivity. [CLS] [SEP] 
  * a session cookie is set to forward the order at our [merchandise shop](/en/merchandise) to our sales partner. [CLS] [SEP] all session cookies are deleted as soon as the user leaves the threema website. [CLS] [SEP] 

#### 4\. [CLS] [SEP] data processed by third parties

the session cookie set by the threema merchandise shop contains the desired
quantity, color and size of an item that is to be ordered and is made
available to our sales partner. [CLS] [SEP] the cookie does not contain any personal data. [CLS] [SEP]

#### 5\. [CLS] [SEP] right to information, correction, blocking, deletion and objection

you have the right to receive information about your personal data stored by
threema gmbh at any time. [CLS] [SEP] likewise, you have the right to correct, block or
(apart from the legally required data storage for business purposes) delete
your personal data. [CLS] [SEP]

#### 6\. [CLS] [SEP] responsible body

if you have any questions about data protection at threema gmbh, you can
contact us directly. [CLS] [SEP] send us an e-mail to
[privacy@threema.ch](mailto:privacy@threema.ch). [CLS] [SEP]

#### 7\. [CLS] [SEP] amendment of the privacy policy

we reserve the right to change this privacy policy from time to time in order
to comply with changed legal requirements or to reflect new functionalities of
the app. [CLS] [SEP] the current privacy policy is always available for consultation from
within threema. [CLS] [SEP]
