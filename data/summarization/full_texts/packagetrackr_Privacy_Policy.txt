# privacy policy

last modified: september 15, 2020

* * *

thanks for visiting our site. [CLS] [SEP] without your trust, we would have to just go
back to tracking our own shipments. [CLS] [SEP] that would make us sad. [CLS] [SEP] so, this page
explains how you can expect us to treat the information that you choose share
with us, and what we collect when you track packages on our site. [CLS] [SEP]

we automatically collect some technical information from your computer when
you visit our site. [CLS] [SEP] this information is provided by your browser and may
include your ip address, os, browser details, and referring site. [CLS] [SEP] to provide
you with tracking information, we query shippers with any tracking number you
provide us. [CLS] [SEP] however, we do not submit any of your personally identifiable
information to any carrier, or other third party. [CLS] [SEP] we respect the
confidentiality of this information and use it exclusively for internal
business purposes, such as administration, security, analysis, and site
development. [CLS] [SEP] we also aggregate this information, so that we can better
understand our site and develop our business. [CLS] [SEP] in aggregation, usage data is
anonymized, and does not allow any detail to be tied to any individual user. [CLS] [SEP]
for instance, we love to brag that we tracked more than 3 million packages
last year; that makes our heads spin!

we provide online customer satisfaction surveys on behalf of our customers. [CLS] [SEP] in
addition to the information we collect through our online survey, we may
request additional personal information, which may be shared with the customer
for whom we provide the survey. [CLS] [SEP] such information might include the survey
participant’s name, telephone number, and email address. [CLS] [SEP] your disclosure of
personal information in our surveys is always optional, but is intended to
allow our customers to follow-up with you in the case of problems with their
service. [CLS] [SEP]

we use the information collected from the online surveys to compile reports on
customer satisfaction for our customers. [CLS] [SEP] due to the sensitive nature of
research data, any information that is collected is treated with the utmost
confidentiality. [CLS] [SEP] we will not sell any personal information gathered from
individual respondents. [CLS] [SEP] all information gathered through our online survey
tool will be the property of packagetrackr llc, and may be licensed to
customers, in an anonymous form. [CLS] [SEP]

we compile data collected from its online surveys to produce benchmark reports
for our customers as well as media and other interested parties. [CLS] [SEP] such
information is based on aggregated, anonymous information and does not include
any personally identifiable information about packagetrackr users. [CLS] [SEP]

we do use cookies to make the site work harder for you, by remembering your
preferences and session details. [CLS] [SEP] you may choose to reject these cookies,
however, in doing so, you will reduce the functionality of the site. [CLS] [SEP]
additionally, google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on
this site. [CLS] [SEP] google uses the doubleclick dart cookie to serve ads to your users
based on their visit to this and other sites on the internet. [CLS] [SEP] you may opt out
of the use of the dart cookie by visiting the google ad and content network
privacy policy. [CLS] [SEP]

when you use our website, we share information that we collect from you, such
as your email (in hashed form), ip address or information about your browser
or operating system, with our partner/service provider, liveramp inc. [CLS] [SEP] liveramp
returns an online identification code that we may store in our first-party
cookie for our use in online and cross-channel advertising and it may be
shared with advertising companies to enable interest-based and targeted
advertising. [CLS] [SEP] to opt out of this use, please click here
(https://optout.liveramp.com/opt_out). [CLS] [SEP]

if you have questions about our privacy policy, please email us at
privacy[at]packagetrackr.com

#### about packagetrackr

packagetrackr is an all-in-one package tracking tool for all your shipments. [CLS] [SEP]
we will remember all of your tracking numbers and pull delivery status
information from dozens of carrier with extra features not offered on the
carriers' sites. [CLS] [SEP]
