# privacy policy

from psychonautwiki

jump to navigation jump to search

at psychonautwiki, your trust means everything to us. [CLS] [SEP]  
  
we do our best to provide an accessible, coherent and private experience for
our users. [CLS] [SEP]  
that’s why we respect your privacy and protect it with strong encryption, plus
strict policies that govern how all data is handled. [CLS] [SEP]  
  
we have spent many thousands of hours engineering specialized software that
protects your privacy. [CLS] [SEP] [you can always individually audit our infrastructure
stack.](https://github.com/psychonautwiki/dmt-infrastructure)  
  
we will never question your motivations, desires and decisions. [CLS] [SEP]  
we deeply believe that using evidence-based information, you can make informed
decisions. [CLS] [SEP]  
  
our commitment to protecting your privacy comes from a deep respect for our
users. [CLS] [SEP]  
we know that your trust doesn’t come easy. [CLS] [SEP]  
  
that’s why we have and always will work as hard as we can to earn and keep it. [CLS] [SEP]

  

![hellooooo.png](https://psychonautwiki.org/w/images/3/3d/xhellooooo.png.pagespeed.ic.rrdxybkz-5.png)

to fulfill this guiding narrative, we obligate ourselves to the following,
legally binding guarantees:

  1. [CLS] [SEP] we will never keep logs at any component of our infrastructure. [CLS] [SEP]
  2. [CLS] [SEP] your ip is anonymized at all times. [CLS] [SEP]
  3. [CLS] [SEP] our servers are physically protected and located in a camera monitored [data center in falkenstein, germany](http://www.datacenterpark.de/) operated by hetzner online. [CLS] [SEP]
  4. [CLS] [SEP] your information is legally protected by german laws. [CLS] [SEP] in germany, gag orders do not exist. [CLS] [SEP] should we ever receive a court order, we will do our best not to comply and publish every single one immediately on the main_page or a designated, from the main_page linked article. [CLS] [SEP] we will at any times promptly inform users targeted by law enforcement or any litigation. [CLS] [SEP]
  5. [CLS] [SEP] we will only publish volunteered information about you and your identity. [CLS] [SEP] we will do our best to comply with deletion requests. [CLS] [SEP]
  6. [CLS] [SEP] we will never sell nor share in any way any information about our users to anyone. [CLS] [SEP]
  7. [CLS] [SEP] we have never worked with any government agency from any country to create a backdoor on our platform. [CLS] [SEP]
  8. [CLS] [SEP] we have also never allowed access to our servers. [CLS] [SEP] and we never will. [CLS] [SEP]
  9. [CLS] [SEP] although every change is recorded and publicly visible, we can update this privacy policy effective immediately at any times without notifying you. [CLS] [SEP]

  

in addition to request processing, your data is used in the following places:

  1. [CLS] [SEP] we use **google analytics** , **facebook analytics** , **yandex.metrika** and **countly** to track usage of our platform, allowing us to edit missing, broken or outdated information and proactively curate content based on usage. [CLS] [SEP]
  2. [CLS] [SEP] from time to time, we may be running private experiments changing the representation or behaviour of the site based on usage. [CLS] [SEP] therefore, parts, or all, of the recorded user data may be used to segment users and derive, induce or deduce, behavioural classes and patterns, reflecting the interests and motivations of a user, that may be used to form meaningful target groups. [CLS] [SEP]
  3. [CLS] [SEP] using collected, anonymized data, we may develop features that take advantage of this and improve the user experience for everyone. [CLS] [SEP]
  4. [CLS] [SEP] we may include anonymized and sampled information in public reports about the progress of psychonautwiki. [CLS] [SEP]
