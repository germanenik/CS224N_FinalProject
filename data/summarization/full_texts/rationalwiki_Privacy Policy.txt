# rationalwiki:privacy policy

from rationalwiki

jump to: navigation, search

  1. [CLS] [SEP] if you are only reading rationalwiki, the only information collected is what is normally collected in server logs. [CLS] [SEP] 
  2. [CLS] [SEP] if you write something on rationalwiki, it's here to stay and will be associated with either your user name or ip address. [CLS] [SEP] 
  3. [CLS] [SEP] if you do not create an account, or fail to log in before editing, your ip address is recorded and made available to everyone reading that edit. [CLS] [SEP] that is why we recommend you create an account and remember to always log in before editing. [CLS] [SEP] 
  4. [CLS] [SEP] if you are logged in with an account then only the information you volunteer about yourself, and your identity, will be made available. [CLS] [SEP] 
  5. [CLS] [SEP] rationalwiki does not sell any information about its users to anyone. [CLS] [SEP] 
  6. [CLS] [SEP] _and_ we will go to the [guantanamo bay](/wiki/guantanamo_bay "guantanamo bay") detention centre _en masse_ as enemy combatants before revealing that information to any government. [CLS] [SEP] unless we _really_ have to. [CLS] [SEP] like pursuant to a subpoena lawfully issued by a court of competent jurisdiction or if we get sued and are required to turn it over in the discovery process. [CLS] [SEP]
