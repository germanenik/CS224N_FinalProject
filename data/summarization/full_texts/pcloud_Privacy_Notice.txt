how we collect, use, protect, and disclose information and data when you use

this privacy policy also explains your choices for managing your information
preferences, including opting out of certain uses of your personal information

use of that personal data differs from the uses disclosed in this privacy
policy. [CLS] [SEP]

we will collect information about you as you use the site or service, such as

we collect information about purchases you've made through our site or
services, such as product type, subscription type, date of purchase, etc. [CLS] [SEP]

paths, as well as information about the timing, frequency and pattern of your

this anonymous information can not be identified directly with you. [CLS] [SEP]
