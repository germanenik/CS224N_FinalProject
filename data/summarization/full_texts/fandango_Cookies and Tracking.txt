## 1\. [CLS] [SEP] what are cookies and how are they used?

like many companies, we use cookies (small text files placed on your computer
or device) and other tracking technologies on the services (referred to
together from this point forward as “cookies”, unless otherwise stated)
including http cookies, html5 and flash local storage/ flash cookies, web
beacons/gifs, embedded scripts, etags/cache browsers, and software development
kits. [CLS] [SEP]

### first party cookies

first party cookies are placed by us (including through the use of third party
service providers) and are used to allow you to use the services and their
features and to assist in analytics activities. [CLS] [SEP]

### third party cookies

certain third parties may place their cookies on your device and use them to
recognize your device when you visit the services and when you visit other
websites or services. [CLS] [SEP] these third parties collect and use this information
pursuant to their own privacy policies. [CLS] [SEP] third party cookies enable certain
features or functionalities and advertising to be provided on the services. [CLS] [SEP]

the services use the following types of first and third party cookies for
these purposes:

  * **strictly necessary cookies:** these cookies are required for service functionality, such as system administration and performance, to assist with fraud deterrence and security, and to enable purchasing capabilities. [CLS] [SEP] because these cookies are necessary, if you refuse them it will impact the functionality of the services. [CLS] [SEP]
  * **information storage and access:** these cookies allow us and our analytic and advertising providers and associates to store and access information on the device, such as device identifiers. [CLS] [SEP]
  * **measurement and analytics:** these cookies collect data regarding your usage of our services and other services, apply market research to generate audiences, and measure the delivery and effectiveness of content and advertising. [CLS] [SEP] we and third parties with whom we work use these cookies to perform analytics, to improve content and user experience, to develop new products and services, and for statistical purposes. [CLS] [SEP] they are also used to recognize you and provide further insights across platforms and devices for the above purposes. [CLS] [SEP] 
  * **personalization cookies:** these cookies enable the services to provide certain features such as determining if you are a first time visitor, remembering choices you have made (such as theaters or places in which you have previously expressed an interest), and assisting you with logging in following registration, including across platforms and devices and placing orders online. [CLS] [SEP] data collected can also be used to select and deliver personalized content to you. [CLS] [SEP] these cookies also allow your device to receive and send information, so you can see and interact with ads and content. [CLS] [SEP] 
    * **content selection and delivery cookies:** data collected under this category can also be used to select and deliver personalized content, such as news articles and videos. [CLS] [SEP]
    * **ad selection and delivery cookies:** these cookies are used to collect data about your browsing habits, your use of the services and other services, your preferences, and your interaction with advertisements across platforms and devices in order to deliver content, including advertisements relevant to your interests. [CLS] [SEP] third party sites and services also use these cookies to deliver content, including advertisements relevant to your interests on the services and third party services. [CLS] [SEP] if you reject these cookies, you may see contextual advertising that may be less relevant to you. [CLS] [SEP]
  * **social media cookies:** these cookies are set by social media services on the services to enable you to share our content with your friends and networks. [CLS] [SEP] these cookies can track your online activity across the services and other services and may impact the content and messages you see on other services you visit. [CLS] [SEP]

we and third parties may associate measurement and analytics cookies,
personalization cookies, content selection and delivery cookies, ad selection
and delivery cookies, and social media cookies with other information we have
about you. [CLS] [SEP]
