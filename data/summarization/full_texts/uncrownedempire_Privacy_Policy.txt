# privacy policy

**online privacy policy agreement**

february 18, 2020

uncrowned empire, llc (uncrowned empire) values its users' privacy. [CLS] [SEP] this
privacy policy ("policy") will help you understand how we collect and use
personal information from those who visit our website or make use of our
online facilities and services, and what we will and will not do with the
information we collect. [CLS] [SEP] our policy has been designed and created to ensure
those affiliated with uncrowned empire, llc of our commitment and realization
of our obligation not only to meet, but to exceed, most existing privacy
standards. [CLS] [SEP]

we reserve the right to make changes to this policy at any given time. [CLS] [SEP] if you
want to make sure that you are up to date with the latest changes, we advise
you to frequently visit this page. [CLS] [SEP] if at any point in time uncrowned empire,
llc decides to make use of any personally identifiable information on file, in
a manner vastly different from that which was stated when this information was
initially collected, the user or users shall be promptly notified by email. [CLS] [SEP]
users at that time shall have the option as to whether to permit the use of
their information in this separate manner. [CLS] [SEP]

this policy applies to uncrowned empire, llc, and it governs any and all data
collection and usage by us. [CLS] [SEP] through the use of
[uncrownedempire.com](https://uncrownedempire.com/), you are therefore
consenting to the data collection procedures expressed in this policy. [CLS] [SEP]

please note that this policy does not govern the collection and use of
information by companies that uncrowned empire, llc does not control, nor by
individuals not employed or managed by us. [CLS] [SEP] if you visit a website that we
mention or link to, be sure to review its privacy policy before providing the
site with information. [CLS] [SEP] it is highly recommended and suggested that you review
the privacy policies and statements of any website you choose to use or
frequent to better understand the way in which websites garner, make use of
and share the information collected. [CLS] [SEP]

specifically, this policy will inform you of the following:

  1. [CLS] [SEP] what personally identifiable information is collected from you through our website; 
  2. [CLS] [SEP] why we collect personally identifiable information and the legal basis for such collection; 
  3. [CLS] [SEP] how we use the collected information and with whom it may be shared; 
  4. [CLS] [SEP] what choices are available to you regarding the use of your data; and 
  5. [CLS] [SEP] the security procedures in place to protect the misuse of your information. [CLS] [SEP] 

**information we collect**

it is always up to you whether to disclose personally identifiable information
to us, although if you elect not to do so, we reserve the right not to
register you as a user or provide you with any products or services. [CLS] [SEP] this
website collects various types of information, such as:

  * voluntarily provided information which may include your name, address, email address, billing and/or credit card information etc. [CLS] [SEP] which may be used when you purchase products and/or services and to deliver the services you have requested. [CLS] [SEP] 
  * information automatically collected when visiting our website, which may include cookies, third party tracking technologies and server logs. [CLS] [SEP] 

in addition, uncrowned empire, llc may have the occasion to collect non-
personal anonymous demographic information, such as age, gender, household
income, political affiliation, race and religion, as well as the type of
browser you are using, ip address, or type of operating system, which will
assist us in providing and maintaining superior quality service. [CLS] [SEP]

uncrowned empire, llc may also deem it necessary, from time to time, to follow
websites that our users may frequent to gleam what types of services and
products may be the most popular to customers or the general public. [CLS] [SEP]

please rest assured that this site will only collect personal information that
you knowingly and willingly provide to us by way of surveys, completed
membership forms, and emails. [CLS] [SEP] it is the intent of this site to use personal
information only for the purpose for which it was requested, and any
additional uses specifically provided for on this policy. [CLS] [SEP]

**why we collect information and for how long**

we are collecting your data for several reasons:

  * to better understand your needs and provide you with the services you have requested; 
  * to fulfill our legitimate interest in improving our services and products; 
  * to send you promotional emails containing information we think you may like when we have your consent to do so; 
  * to contact you to fill out surveys or participate in other types of market research, when we have your consent to do so; 
  * to customize our website according to your online behavior and personal preferences. [CLS] [SEP] 

the data we collect from you will be stored for no longer than necessary. [CLS] [SEP] the
length of time we retain said information will be determined based upon the
following criteria: the length of time your personal information remains
relevant; the length of time it is reasonable to keep records to demonstrate
that we have fulfilled our duties and obligations; any limitation periods
within which claims might be made; any retention periods prescribed by law or
recommended by regulators, professional bodies or associations; the type of
contract we have with you, the existence of your consent, and our legitimate
interest in keeping such information as stated in this policy. [CLS] [SEP]

**use of information collected**

uncrowned empire, llc does not now, nor will it in the future, sell, rent or
lease any of its customer lists and/or names to any third parties. [CLS] [SEP]

uncrowned empire, llc may collect and may make use of personal information to
assist in the operation of our website and to ensure delivery of the services
you need and request. [CLS] [SEP] at times, we may find it necessary to use personally
identifiable information as a means to keep you informed of other possible
products and/or services that may be available to you from
uncrownedempire.com/

uncrowned empire, llc may also be in contact with you with regards to
completing surveys and/or research questionnaires related to your opinion of
current or potential future services that may be offered. [CLS] [SEP]

uncrowned empire, llc uses various third-party social media features including
but not limited to facebook, twitter, instagram, linkedin, youtube and other
interactive programs. [CLS] [SEP] these may collect your ip address and require cookies to
work properly. [CLS] [SEP] these services are governed by the privacy policies of the
providers and are not within uncrowned empire, llc's control. [CLS] [SEP]

**disclosure of information**

uncrowned empire, llc may not use or disclose the information provided by you
except under the following circumstances:

  * as necessary to provide services or products you have ordered; 
  * in other ways described in this policy or to which you have otherwise consented; 
  * in the aggregate with other information in such a way so that your identity cannot reasonably be determined; 
  * as required by law, or in response to a subpoena or search warrant; 
  * to outside auditors who have agreed to keep the information confidential; 
  * as necessary to enforce the terms of service; 
  * as necessary to maintain, safeguard and preserve all the rights and property of uncrowned empire, llc. [CLS] [SEP] 

**non-marketing purposes**

uncrowned empire, llc greatly respects your privacy. [CLS] [SEP] we do maintain and
reserve the right to contact you if needed for non-marketing purposes (such as
bug alerts, security breaches, account issues, and/or changes in uncrowned
empire, llc products and services). [CLS] [SEP] in certain circumstances, we may use our
website, newspapers, or other public means to post a notice. [CLS] [SEP]

**children under the age of 13**

uncrowned empire, llc's website is not directed to, and does not knowingly
collect personal identifiable information from, children under the age of
thirteen (13). [CLS] [SEP] if it is determined that such information has been
inadvertently collected on anyone under the age of thirteen (13), we shall
immediately take the necessary steps to ensure that such information is
deleted from our system's database, or in the alternative, that verifiable
parental consent is obtained for the use and storage of such information. [CLS] [SEP]
anyone under the age of thirteen (13) must seek and obtain parent or guardian
permission to use this website. [CLS] [SEP]

storage and use as described in this policy. [CLS] [SEP]

your rights as a data subject

under the regulations of the general data protection regulation ("gdpr") of
the eu you have certain rights as a data subject. [CLS] [SEP] these rights are as follows:

  * **the right to be informed** : this means we must inform you of how we intend to use your personal data and we do this through the terms of this policy. [CLS] [SEP] 
  * **the right of access** : this means you have the right to request access to the data we hold about you and we must respond to those requests within one month. [CLS] [SEP] you can do this by emailing [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)
  * **the right to rectification:** this means that if you believe some of the date, we hold is incorrect, you have the right to have it corrected. [CLS] [SEP] you can do this by logging into your account with us, or by sending us an email with your request. [CLS] [SEP] 
  * **the right to erasure** : this means you can request that the information we hold be deleted, and we will comply unless we have a compelling reason not to, in which case you will be informed of same. [CLS] [SEP] you can do this by sending an email to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)
  * **the right to restrict processing** : this means you can change your communication preferences or opt-out of certain communications. [CLS] [SEP] you can do this by sending an email to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)
  * **the right of data portability:** this means you can obtain and use the data we hold for your own purposes without explanation. [CLS] [SEP] if you wish to request a copy of your information, contact us at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)
  * **the right to object** : this means you can file a formal objection with us regarding our use of your information with regard to third parties, or its processing where our legal basis is our legitimate interest in it. [CLS] [SEP] to do this, please send an email to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)

in addition to the rights above, please rest assured that we will always aim
to encrypt and anonymize your personal information whenever possible. [CLS] [SEP] we also
have protocols in place in the unlikely event that we suffer a data breach and
we will contact you if your personal information is ever at risk. [CLS] [SEP] for more
details regarding our security protections see the section below or visit our
website at uncrownedempire.com. [CLS] [SEP]

**security**

uncrowned empire, llc takes precautions to protect your information. [CLS] [SEP] when you
submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected
both online and offline. [CLS] [SEP] wherever we collect sensitive information (e.g. [CLS] [SEP]
credit card information), that information is encrypted and transmitted to us
in a secure way. [CLS] [SEP] you can verify this by looking for a lock icon in the address
bar and looking for "https" at the beginning of the address of the webpage. [CLS] [SEP]

while we use encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online,
we also protect your information offline. [CLS] [SEP] only employees who need the
information to perform a specific job (for example, billing or customer
service) are granted access to personally identifiable information. [CLS] [SEP] the
computers and servers in which we store personally identifiable information
are kept in a secure environment. [CLS] [SEP] this is all done to prevent any loss,
misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure or modification of the user's personal
information under our control. [CLS] [SEP]

the company also uses secure socket layer (ssl) for authentication and private
communications to build users' trust and confidence in the internet and
website use by providing simple and secure access and communication of credit
card and personal information. [CLS] [SEP]
