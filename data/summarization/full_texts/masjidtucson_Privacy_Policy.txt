# app privacy policy, god willing

* * *

masjid tucson does not directly collect any personally identifiable
information about you through this app. [CLS] [SEP]

any information we do have about app usage will never be sold or rented to
others, god willing (i.e., it is for internal use). [CLS] [SEP]

god willing, we will not share your information without consent to a third
party outside our organization except for the following reason. [CLS] [SEP] we may share
the information collected with an external entity if we have a good-faith
belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of the information is
reasonably necessary to:

  * meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request. [CLS] [SEP]
  * detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues. [CLS] [SEP]
  * protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of our organization, our users or the public as required or permitted by law. [CLS] [SEP]
