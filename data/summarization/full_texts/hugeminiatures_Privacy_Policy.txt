# privacy policy

# privacy policy

this policy describes the information we collect and use when you visit or
make a purchase from our site. [CLS] [SEP] it also covers how that information is used,
and your options regarding what happens with the information that you provide
us. [CLS] [SEP]

### personal information

if you make a transaction on our site, there’s some personal information
that’s required to fulfill your order. [CLS] [SEP] this includes your name, email, phone
number, billing/shipping address, and payment information. [CLS] [SEP] we use this order
information to process your payment, arrange for shipping, and provide you
with confirmation and shipping details. [CLS] [SEP] we use stripe and paypal for payment
processing, who may alternatively collect and store payment transaction data
on their own servers. [CLS] [SEP]

### cookies

we use cookies (a small piece of data transferred to your device) to remember
information such as items added to the shopping cart. [CLS] [SEP] they are also used for
record keeping. [CLS] [SEP] you may deactivate or restrict the sending of cookies in your
web browser settings. [CLS] [SEP] cookies that are already stored may be deleted at any
time. [CLS] [SEP]

### tracking

we use anonymized data to improve our site. [CLS] [SEP] we will store the page from which
you visited us from, the parts of our site you visit, the date and duration of
your visit, and information about the device that was used during your visit. [CLS] [SEP]
we do not allow third party tracker networks to record your activity on this
site. [CLS] [SEP]

### opt-out of website tracking

### email

if you subscribe to our email list using a newsletter form, or opt-in during
the checkout step, you can expect to receive news or promotional email
communications. [CLS] [SEP] if you no longer wish to be included, you can click the
unsubscribe link in any of the email messages, or remove yourself
[here](https://hugeminis.us17.list-
manage.com/unsubscribe?u=a42d32d7c88602743887d93e9&id=55882e5be7). [CLS] [SEP]

### retention of data

the information covered here is retained on a secure server for use by us as
reference. [CLS] [SEP] none of the personal information obtained by us will ever be sold,
intentionally made public, or used in any way other than what’s listed. [CLS] [SEP] you
can contact us in regards to the personal information we may hold about you
and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. [CLS] [SEP]
if you wish to do this, please contact us [here](/contact-us/). [CLS] [SEP]

### tl;dr

we respect your privacy and do our utmost to make sure that your personal
information remains private. [CLS] [SEP] we have never, and will never sell or
intentionally make public, any personal information gathered on this site. [CLS] [SEP]
