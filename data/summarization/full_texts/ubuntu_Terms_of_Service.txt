# legal terms and conditions

we have a number of legal agreements to protect your personal data and the
contributions you make to the projects we manage. [CLS] [SEP] we also have agreements that
enable us to share intellectual property (which includes our trademarks and
content as well as code), plus legal descriptions of exactly what our support
services cover. [CLS] [SEP]
