# cookies used by the ripe ncc

list of cookies used by the ripe ncc

### **cookie name**

|

### **purpose**

|

### **term**  
  
---|---|---  
  
**serverid**

**jsessionid**

**phpsessid**

|

these cookie identifies the user. [CLS] [SEP] it is used for tying the user session to a
particular application server. [CLS] [SEP] this cookie is set when you visit any of our
pages being served from our content management systems. [CLS] [SEP] it is used to help our
server to handle your requests in the most efficient way and is deleted when
you quit your browser. [CLS] [SEP]

|

expires when the browser session is closed. [CLS] [SEP]  
  
**crowd.token_key**

|

this cookie identifies the user. [CLS] [SEP] it is used to authenticate logged-in single-
sign-on (ripe ncc access) users. [CLS] [SEP]



|

expires when the browser session is closed  
  
**_pk_id.4.1234**

|

this cookie is used to keep track of traffic for analytic purposes when
accessing a ripe meeting website. [CLS] [SEP]



|

expires after 2 years  
  
**_pk_ses.4.1234**

|

this cookie is used to keep track traffic for analytic purposes upon accessing
ripe meeting sites. [CLS] [SEP] this information is not shared with third parties. [CLS] [SEP]



|

expires after 30 minutes  
  
**lastknownlocationlng**

**lastknownlocationlat**

**lastknownlocationzoom**



|

this cookie saves the state of the lir locator application. [CLS] [SEP] when the user
opens the page again, they will find the same location they had in their
previous visit. [CLS] [SEP]

|

expires after 3 months  
  
**m2mtermsaccepted**

|

this cookie is set when users accept terms of service or terms and conditions
of a service. [CLS] [SEP] if this cookie is not set, users will be asked to accept terms
every time before they use a ripe ncc service. [CLS] [SEP]



|

expires after 3 months  
  
**firstvisit-beta**

|

this cookie prevents help information from appearing again after the user has
discarded it. [CLS] [SEP]



|

expires after 3 months  
  
**stat-session**



**atlasessp**



|

these are standard cookies for user identification and session persistence. [CLS] [SEP]



|

expires after 2 weeks  
  
**stat-csrftoken**



**csrftoken**



|

these cookies enhance security to protect from cross-site scripting attacks. [CLS] [SEP]
presently they are used to support comments forms. [CLS] [SEP]



|

expires after 1 year  
  
**crowd.ripe.hint**

|

this cookie is used to identify logged in users among ripe ncc services
(single sign on). [CLS] [SEP]



|

expires when the browser session is closed  
  
**pref**

|

this cookie is used for youtube services. [CLS] [SEP] it is handling user preferences. [CLS] [SEP] it
is beyond the ripe ncc's control. [CLS] [SEP] use of youtube services like embedded video
require that the user request those services from youtube directly, which in
