## cookies

we use cookies to keep you logged in and to keep your settings. [CLS] [SEP] to make things
quicker, and to give better recommendations, stuff such as tags (keywords) of
the videos you watch are saved in the session cookie, but is not being saved
elsewhere. [CLS] [SEP]

you're not being tracked, we do not sell anything about you. [CLS] [SEP]

## data

we only save your login and what you own, likes and dislikes (when pressing
those buttons), you can delete account in [settings](../settings). [CLS] [SEP] below is a
more detailed list. [CLS] [SEP]

## required:

### stored physically

  * mail
  * name
  * password
  * channel id
  * date when account was created. [CLS] [SEP]

### stored temporary

  * ipaddress on signup to prevent account spam. [CLS] [SEP]

## optional:

### stored physically

  * icon / channel avatar
  * subscriptions
  * liked videos
  * disliked videos
  * uploaded content
  * history - [settings](/settings)

## cookie session (stored temporary)

  * if current video has been seen. [CLS] [SEP]
  * channel information (id, icon) to smoothen loading times. [CLS] [SEP]
  * which tags the videos you've seen has had so that they can be used in recommendations. [CLS] [SEP]

  

### third parties

only ads are from third-parties, it's how we and our creators get paid. [CLS] [SEP] no
third parties gets your data. [CLS] [SEP]
