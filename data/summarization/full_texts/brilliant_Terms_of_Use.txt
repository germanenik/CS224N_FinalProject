# brilliant cookie policy

_effective: may 21, 2018_

please remember that your use of the services is at all times subject to the
[terms of use](/terms-of-use/) and [privacy policy](/privacy/), which
incorporates this cookie policy. [CLS] [SEP] any terms we use in this cookie policy
without defining them have the definitions given to them in the [terms of
use](/terms-of-use/) and [privacy policy](/privacy/). [CLS] [SEP]

## cookie policy

the services use cookies and similar technologies such as pixel tags, web
beacons, clear gifs, javascript, and local storage. [CLS] [SEP] cookies are small files –
usually consisting of letters and numbers – placed on your computer, tablet,
phone, or similar device, when you use that device to visit a site. [CLS] [SEP] we will
refer to cookies and similar technologies simply as "cookies" for the
remainder of this cookie policy. [CLS] [SEP] we use cookies to enable our servers to
recognize your web browser and tell us how and when you visit and otherwise
use the services through the internet, to analyze trends, and to learn about
our user base. [CLS] [SEP]

we may combine non-personally identifiable information collected through
cookies with other personal information that we have about you, for example,
to tell us who you are or whether you have an account. [CLS] [SEP] we may also supplement
the information we collect from you with information received from third
parties in order to enhance our services, or to offer you information that we
believe may be of interest to you. [CLS] [SEP] where we use cookies to collect information
that is personally identifiable, or that can become personally identifiable if
we combine it with other information, the brilliant [privacy
policy](/privacy/) will apply in addition to this cookie policy. [CLS] [SEP]

you can control and/or delete cookies as you wish – for details, see
[aboutcookies.org](https://www.aboutcookies.org/). [CLS] [SEP] most browsers have an
option for turning off the cookie feature, which will prevent your browser
from accepting new cookies, as well as (depending on the sophistication of
your browser software) allowing you to decide on acceptance of each new cookie
in a variety of ways. [CLS] [SEP] you can delete all cookies that are already on your
computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. [CLS] [SEP] if
you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every
time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work. [CLS] [SEP] we
strongly recommend that you leave cookies active, because they enable you to
take advantage of the most attractive features of the services. [CLS] [SEP]

further information on cookies can be found  at
<http://ec.europa.eu/ipg/basics/legal/cookies/index_en.htm>. [CLS] [SEP]

## what types of cookies do we use and for which purposes?

we use the following types of cookies:

### essential cookies

these are cookies that are required to provide you with features or services
that you have requested. [CLS] [SEP] for example, cookies that enable you to log into
secure areas of our site or use a shopping cart. [CLS] [SEP] disabling these cookies will
encumber the services’ performance and may make certain features and services
unavailable. [CLS] [SEP]

### analytical cookies

these cookies allow us to understand how visitors use our site. [CLS] [SEP] this includes
information about the number of visitors to the site, what pages visitors view
on our site and how long visitors are viewing pages on the site. [CLS] [SEP] analytical
cookies also help us measure the performance of our advertising campaigns in
order to help us improve our advertising campaigns and to optimize our
services’ content for those who engage with our advertising. [CLS] [SEP]

### third party cookies

enabled

these are cookies placed by a third party on your device and may provide
information to those third parties about browsing habits (including your
visits to our services, the pages you have visited and the links and
advertisements you have clicked) that may help identify if certain services
that such third parties provide are being used, identify your interests,
retarget you and serve advertisements that are relevant to you. [CLS] [SEP] we do not have
control or access to such cookies. [CLS] [SEP] if you look at one page on our services, an
advertisement may be delivered to you on our services or on other sites, for
products referenced on that page or for similar products and services. [CLS] [SEP] we do
not engage in retargeted advertising for children users of our services. [CLS] [SEP]

### session cookies and persistent cookies

the types of cookies described above may be "session cookies" which are
temporary cookies that are only stored on your device while you are visiting
our site or "persistent cookies" which are cookies that are stored on your
device for a period of time after you leave our site. [CLS] [SEP] the length of time a
persistent cookie stays on your device varies from cookie to cookie. [CLS] [SEP] we use
persistent cookies to store your preferences so that they are available for
the next visit, and to keep a more accurate account of how often you visit our
services, how often you return, how your use of the services may vary over
time, and measure the effectiveness of advertising efforts. [CLS] [SEP] through these
cookies, we may collect information about your online activity after you leave
our services. [CLS] [SEP] just like any other usage information we collect, this
information allows us to improve the services and customize your online
experience. [CLS] [SEP] your browser may offer you a "do not track" option, which allows
you to signal to operators of websites and web applications and services
(including behavioral advertising services) that you do not wish such
operators to track certain of your online activities over time and across
different websites. [CLS] [SEP] our services do not support do not track requests at this
time, which means that we collect information about your online activity both
while you are using the services and after you leave the services. [CLS] [SEP]

## cookie settings

you can decide whether or not to accept cookies. [CLS] [SEP] one way you can do this is
through your internet browser’s settings. [CLS] [SEP] to find our more information about
cookies, including information about how to manage and delete cookies, please
visit <https://ico.org.uk/for-the-public/online/cookies/> or
<http://www.allaboutcookies.org/>. [CLS] [SEP]

to explore what cookie setting are available to you, look in the "preferences"
or "options" section of your browser's menu. [CLS] [SEP]
