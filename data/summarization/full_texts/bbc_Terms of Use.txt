# a few rules for us and you

we want everyone to enjoy the bbc. [CLS] [SEP] but there are a few rules to stick to. [CLS] [SEP]

take a look through our explainers to see what you (and we) can and can't do. [CLS] [SEP]
our explainers cover questions we often get asked. [CLS] [SEP] can't find what you're
after? take a look at the full version of the terms of use. [CLS] [SEP]
