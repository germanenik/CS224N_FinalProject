## introduction

listudy.org (“listudy”) is an online chess site. [CLS] [SEP] users can optionally register
for an account; however, certain features are available for registered users
only. [CLS] [SEP] this is because listudy needs basic information from its users in order
to function. [CLS] [SEP]

this privacy policy will detail:

  1. [CLS] [SEP] what information listudy collects
  2. [CLS] [SEP] why this information is collected and how it's used
  3. [CLS] [SEP] how cookies are used
  4. [CLS] [SEP] managing you information
  5. [CLS] [SEP] storage of the collected information
  6. [CLS] [SEP] colsing your account
  7. [CLS] [SEP] other

## what information listudy collects

information that listudy collects:

**user submitted information**

if you create an account, you will be asked to provide a email address. [CLS] [SEP] this
email address does not have to be a real email address. [CLS] [SEP]

**technical information**

when you visit listudy, some information about the request is logged on the
server. [CLS] [SEP] this information includes the time of the request, the http method,
the uri of the request, the status code generated by the server and the
referrer of the request. [CLS] [SEP]

## why this information is collected and how it’s used

**user submitted information**

your email address will remain confidential and is used to log in or in case
you forget your password. [CLS] [SEP] moderators are able to set email addresses for
accounts to assist in troubleshooting. [CLS] [SEP]

**technical information**

this information is stored to detect attacks and malicious access to the site. [CLS] [SEP]

**disclosing your information**

your information will only be disclosed if it is legally necessary to do so. [CLS] [SEP]
for example, if lichess is ordered to hand over a certain user’s information,
or if someone hacks lichess. [CLS] [SEP]

if listudy is sold, the data provided by the user (such as emails, pgns,
comments) will be transferred to a new third party owner. [CLS] [SEP]

## cookies

a cookie is a text string that listudy stores in your browser in order to keep
you logged in throughout sessions and save your site preferences. [CLS] [SEP]

## managing your information

you can edit or delete your user provided informations at any time. [CLS] [SEP]

information you delete is deleted from the live site but remains in listudy’
backups for up to one year. [CLS] [SEP]

your technical informations are needed for administrative purposes and cannot
be deleted. [CLS] [SEP]

## storage of the collected information

collected information is stored on listudy’ servers, which are hosted by
digitalocean. [CLS] [SEP]

## closing your account

you may choose to close your account at any time, for any reason. [CLS] [SEP]

send me an email if you want to close your account. [CLS] [SEP]

## other

listudy can change this policy at its discretion. [CLS] [SEP]
