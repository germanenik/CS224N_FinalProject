# at&t privacy policy

![privacy image](/ecms/dam/csr/privacy/privacy.png)

whenever you do something like buy one of our products, watch a show or
download an app, information is created. [CLS] [SEP] because we know your privacy is
important, we have a privacy policy to explain how we collect, use and protect
that information. [CLS] [SEP]



our policy is designed to provide transparency into our privacy practices, in
a format that is easy to read, understand and navigate through your various
choices. [CLS] [SEP] we want to simplify this explanation, so you can make informed
choices about your privacy. [CLS] [SEP] for a quick summary, hover over each section or
click the link to take you to the actual section of the privacy policy. [CLS] [SEP]

_icon-info_

## introduction

the at&t privacy policy describes the information we collect, use and share
and provides the choices and controls available. [CLS] [SEP]

[more _arrow right_](/csr/privacy/full_privacy_policy)

_icon-content-filled_

## the information we collect

we collect information about how you’re using our network, products, and
services. [CLS] [SEP]

[more _arrow right_](/csr/privacy/full_privacy_policy#collect-information)

_icon-cloud_

## how we collect your information

we collect information in 3 key ways: 1.) when you give it to us; 2.) when we
automatically get it when you use our products; and 3.) when we get it from
outside sources

[more _arrow right_](/csr/privacy/full_privacy_policy#how-collect)

_icon-connections_

## how we use your information

we use your information for various things like managing our business,
providing insights to our business customers, serving advertising, and
improving your experience. [CLS] [SEP]

[more _arrow right_](/csr/privacy/full_privacy_policy#how-use)

_icon-sharing_

## how we share your information

we share your information from time to time, within specific rules. [CLS] [SEP] for
example, we may share it with our affiliates and other companies to provide
you the best experience. [CLS] [SEP]

[more _arrow right_](/csr/privacy/full_privacy_policy#how-share)

_icon-gears_

## your privacy choices and controls

we offer you choices about how we contact you and use your information. [CLS] [SEP] you
also have choices about how certain third parties and advertisers use your
information. [CLS] [SEP]

# we've updated our privacy policy

on june 19th, we updated the section of our privacy policy on the california
consumer privacy act. [CLS] [SEP]  


  * we added a description of how we verify your identity before processing your request to access or delete your data. [CLS] [SEP]
  * we added information about sources of data we collect and with whom we may share it. [CLS] [SEP]
  * we describe how you can designate an authorized agent to make your requests. [CLS] [SEP]
