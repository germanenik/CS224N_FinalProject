# privacy and cookies policy

**effective date: 10 may 2018.**

1\. [CLS] [SEP] **who are we?**

1.1 we are pythonanywhere llp. [CLS] [SEP] you can contact us at
support@pythonanywhere.com. [CLS] [SEP] other contact details are on our website. [CLS] [SEP]

2\. [CLS] [SEP] **what's the point of this policy?**

2.1 it tells you what to expect when we collect your personal information. [CLS] [SEP]
please only use our service if you are completely happy with this policy. [CLS] [SEP]

2.2. [CLS] [SEP] generally, the policy covers only information provided to us. [CLS] [SEP] it doesn't
cover personal information which you give to other people, such as payment
providers or other websites. [CLS] [SEP] if you use them, please check their privacy
policies. [CLS] [SEP] nor does this policy apply to personal information of other people
(e.g. [CLS] [SEP] customers) collected by users of our services. [CLS] [SEP] we just act as "data
processors"; if necessary, please contact the service users, who are the "data
controllers". [CLS] [SEP]

3\. [CLS] [SEP] **might the policy change?**

3.1 yes. [CLS] [SEP] please check whenever you visit our website. [CLS] [SEP] we will assume you agree
to the new version of the policy if you use the site after its effective date. [CLS] [SEP]

4\. [CLS] [SEP] **what do we collect?**

4.1 information which you upload to our service or otherwise give us such as
your name, username, email address and other contact details as well as any
personal information which you include in public activities on our service,
e.g. [CLS] [SEP] forum posts. [CLS] [SEP]

4.2 your billing address as well as the last four digits and expiry date of
your credit card, which may be sent to us by our payment providers. [CLS] [SEP]

4.3 automated information such as the internet protocol (ip) address used to
connect your device to the internet, connection information such as browser
type and version, information about your device including device-type and
device identifier, operating system and platform, mobile network data, a
unique reference number linked to the data you enter on our system, login
details, the site from which you arrived at our service, details of your
activity with date / time stamps including pages you visited and your searches
/ transactions. [CLS] [SEP]

4.4 personal information of your customers or others which you choose to store
on our servers in connection with our supply of services. [CLS] [SEP]

5\. [CLS] [SEP] **what's our reason / legal basis for collecting the information?**

5.1 because it's necessary to provide you with our services under our contract
with you. [CLS] [SEP] we may use it for other purposes (such as email marketing) if you
consent. [CLS] [SEP] in the case of automated information collected when you browse our
site, we use this to manage and improve our service in accordance with our
"legitimate interests". [CLS] [SEP]

6\. [CLS] [SEP] **what about cookies?**

6.1 we and/or third parties use cookies and other tracking technologies on our
website. [CLS] [SEP] a cookie is an identifier (a small file of letters and numbers) that
is sent to your computer. [CLS] [SEP] our website's functionality will be limited if you
configure your browser to reject cookies. [CLS] [SEP]

6.2 cookies are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently,
as well as to provide information to the website owner or others. [CLS] [SEP] session
cookies are temporary cookies that remain in the cookie file of your browser
only until your browser is closed. [CLS] [SEP] they allow websites to link your actions
during a browser session. [CLS] [SEP] persistent cookies stay in the cookie file of your
browser for longer (how long will depend on the lifetime of the specific
cookie). [CLS] [SEP] for further information on cookies, including how to use your browser
to block them and how to delete cookies already stored on your device, visit:
[www.allaboutcookies.org](http://www.allaboutcookies.org). [CLS] [SEP]

6.3 the following kinds of cookies may be used on this website:

a) **session cookies** these enable us to keep track of your movement from
page to page and store your selections so you do not get asked repeatedly for
the same information. [CLS] [SEP] they allow you to proceed through many pages of the site
quickly and easily without having to authenticate or reprocess each new area
you visit. [CLS] [SEP] for example, a session cookie remembers your shopping cart
selection so you will have the items you selected when you are ready to check
out. [CLS] [SEP]

b) **operational cookies:** we use persistent cookies (up to 12 months) to
manage sessions on our service including to help with logging in and out and
security as well as to track whether you visited us from an affiliate site. [CLS] [SEP]

c) **google cookies:** persistent cookies (up to four years we believe) are
set in connection with the following google services on our site and these
cookies may involve certain information (such as your ip address and web
address of the page you're visiting) being sent to google:

visit such as duration, route through the website and what sites the visitor
came from. [CLS] [SEP] this information helps us to improve the way our website works, for
example by making sure users find what they need easily. [CLS] [SEP] you can specifically
opt out of ga [here](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/). [CLS] [SEP] see below for
other opt out routes. [CLS] [SEP]

account is closed -- for tax reasons and/or to help deal with any disputes. [CLS] [SEP]

until you tell us to stop sending you marketing messages (or your account is
closed, if that happens sooner). [CLS] [SEP]
