evaluation criteria prior to posting a link, the city of reading, pa evaluates
the link to determine if it meets one of the following criteria:

  * the link is to an official u.s. [CLS] [SEP] federal government web site. [CLS] [SEP]
  * the link is to a web site that provides official u.s. [CLS] [SEP] government information or services. [CLS] [SEP]
  * the link is to a web site with content that complements existing information, products, and services on readingpa.gov where it is posted. [CLS] [SEP]

in addition, all links on readingpa.gov must meet all of the following
criteria

  * the web site being linked has content that is relevant, useful, and authoritative for citizens, businesses, and/or government officials. [CLS] [SEP]
  * the web site being linked has information that appears to be accurate and current. [CLS] [SEP] 
  * the web site being linked has a posted privacy policy that clearly describes the organization’s information handling practices. [CLS] [SEP]
  * the web site being linked is "user-friendly”. [CLS] [SEP] 

**links to government web sites**

readingpa.gov may post links to any government web site that is publicly
available unless directed not to by the agency that owns the site. [CLS] [SEP] federal
government-owned or sponsored web sites will generally have .gov, .mil or
.fed.us domains, however, some may occasionally end in .com, .org or .net. [CLS] [SEP]
readingpa.gov may also link to web sites created by public or private sector
partnerships with the federal government, and state and local government web
sites. [CLS] [SEP]

**links to non-government web sites**

in rare instances, readingpa.gov will contain links to non-government owned or
sponsored web sites, provided these sites provide government information
and/or services in a way that is not available on an official government web
site. [CLS] [SEP] readingpa.gov provides these links as a public service to allow web
visitors to obtain information or services related to the site they are
visiting. [CLS] [SEP] the city of reading, pa, neither endorses nor guarantees in any way
the external organizations, services, advice, or products included in these
web site links. [CLS] [SEP]

furthermore, the city of reading, pa neither controls nor guarantees the
accuracy, relevance, timeliness, or completeness of the information contained
in non-government web site links. [CLS] [SEP] (see disclaimer of endorsement for more
information on this topic.)

**prohibitions**

readingpa.gov will not approve a link to any web site that exhibits hate,
bias, or discrimination. [CLS] [SEP] furthermore, the city of reading, pa reserves the
right to deny or remove any link that contains misleading information or
unsubstantiated claims, or is determined to be in conflict with the city of
reading, pa mission or policies. [CLS] [SEP]

**disclaimer of endorsement**

the information posted on readingpa.gov may include hypertext links or
pointers to information created and maintained by other public and/or private
organizations. [CLS] [SEP] readingpa.gov provides these links and pointers solely for our
users' information and convenience. [CLS] [SEP] when users select a link to an outside web
site, they are leaving the readingpa.gov web site and are subject to the
privacy and security policies of the owners/sponsors of the outside web site. [CLS] [SEP]

the city of reading, pa does not control or guarantee the accuracy, relevance,
timeliness, or completeness of information contained on a linked web site. [CLS] [SEP]

the city of reading, pa does not endorse the organizations sponsoring linked
web sites, and we do not endorse the views they express or the
products/services they offer. [CLS] [SEP]

the city of reading, pa cannot authorize the use of copyrighted materials
contained in linked web sites. [CLS] [SEP] users must request such authorization from the
sponsor of the linked web site. [CLS] [SEP]

the city of reading, pa is not responsible for transmissions users receive
from linked web sites. [CLS] [SEP]
