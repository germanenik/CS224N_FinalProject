## log files

join run follows a standard procedure of using log files. [CLS] [SEP] these files log
visitors when they visit websites. [CLS] [SEP] all hosting companies do this and a part of
hosting services' analytics. [CLS] [SEP] the information collected by log files include
internet protocol (ip) addresses, browser type, internet service provider
(isp), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages. [CLS] [SEP] these are not linked to any
information that is personally identifiable. [CLS] [SEP] the purpose of the information is
for analyzing issues and administering the site. [CLS] [SEP]

## ccpa privacy rights (do not sell my personal information)

under the ccpa, among other rights, california consumers have the right to:

request that a business that collects a consumer's personal data disclose the
categories and specific pieces of personal data that a business has collected
about consumers. [CLS] [SEP]

request that a business delete any personal data about the consumer that a
business has collected. [CLS] [SEP]

request that a business that sells a consumer's personal data, not sell the
consumer's personal data. [CLS] [SEP]

if you make a request, we have one month to respond to you, but we will aim to
respond within a day. [CLS] [SEP] if you would like to exercise any of these rights,
please contact us. [CLS] [SEP]

## gdpr data protection rights

we would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection
rights. [CLS] [SEP] every user is entitled to the following:

the right to access – you have the right to request copies of your personal
data. [CLS] [SEP] we may charge you a small fee for this service. [CLS] [SEP]

the right to rectification – you have the right to request that we correct any
information you believe is inaccurate. [CLS] [SEP] you also have the right to request that
we complete the information you believe is incomplete. [CLS] [SEP]

the right to erasure – you have the right to request that we erase your
personal data, under certain conditions. [CLS] [SEP]

the right to restrict processing – you have the right to request that we
restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions. [CLS] [SEP]

the right to object to processing – you have the right to object to our
processing of your personal data, under certain conditions. [CLS] [SEP]

the right to data portability – you have the right to request that we transfer
the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you,
under certain conditions. [CLS] [SEP]

if you make a request, we have one month to respond to you, but we will aim to
respond within a day. [CLS] [SEP] if you would like to exercise any of these rights,
please contact us. [CLS] [SEP]
