## privacy policy

![privacy icon](img/privacy.png)

### learn how we protect your privacy. [CLS] [SEP]

## in a nutshell... [CLS] [SEP]

windowslogic productions cares about your privacy and likes to let you know
what data we have or haven't got about you. [CLS] [SEP] in a nutshell, we do not collect
any data which you don't want and dispose of data after a 30 day period if you
do want us to use your data. [CLS] [SEP] your data is used to improve our website,
applications and services so we can provide you with the best possible
experience. [CLS] [SEP]

## 1\. [CLS] [SEP] sharing information

windowslogic productions does not share any personal information you give us
with anyone. [CLS] [SEP] ip addresses and domains are kept private, but can be reported to
authorities if they are attacking the services directly. [CLS] [SEP]

## 2\. [CLS] [SEP] information collection & your rights

when contacting us or sending a form on our site, as appropriate, you may be
asked to enter your name, email address and other details to help you with
your experience, this data is recorded and sent to us but is deleted after it
has been read and/or dealt with and kept for no more than a maximum of 30 days
if specified. [CLS] [SEP]

### right of subject access

if you wish to access or export any of your personal data, then please
[contact us](contact.php). [CLS] [SEP] your data will be sent to the email provided within
24 hours. [CLS] [SEP]

### right to be informed

you have the right to be informed about any data collection and use of
personal data, the reasons for processing it and who can see it. [CLS] [SEP]

### right of rectification

you have the right to ask for data to be put right if the data is inaccurate
or incomplete. [CLS] [SEP]

### right of erasure

you have the right to request if information is removed if it is no longer
needed or used. [CLS] [SEP]

### right to restrict processing

the right in some curcumstances, to prevent the processing of personal
information if it will cause damage or distress. [CLS] [SEP]

### right to object

the right to object to the processing of information (unless legitimate
grounds for the processing are demonstrated that over-ride the interests,
rights and freedom of the individual.)

### right to portability

the right to receive information in an electronic format. [CLS] [SEP]

### other information

windowslogic productions will endeavour to ensure that information is kept
accurate at all times. [CLS] [SEP]

we place a high value on the importance of information security and have a
number of procedures in place to minimise the possibility of a compromise in
data security. [CLS] [SEP]

## 3\. [CLS] [SEP] using "cookies"

we do not use cookies for tracking purposes, session or any type of data
collection, analytics or statistics. [CLS] [SEP]

## 4\. [CLS] [SEP] third party disclosure

we do not send any information to third parties or get information from any
third parties, all data put into this website is completely private and no one
has the ability to look or get hold of it. [CLS] [SEP] all data is encrypted in
aes-256-cbc and is kept local and based around the windowslogic productions
servers, no personal data is leaked by us and is confidential. [CLS] [SEP]

## 5\. [CLS] [SEP] third party links

occasionally, at our discretion, we may include or offer third party products
or services on our website. [CLS] [SEP] these third party products or services have
separate and independent privacy policies. [CLS] [SEP] we therefore have no responsibility
or liability for the content and activities of these linked products or
services. [CLS] [SEP] nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and
welcome any feedback about these products or services. [CLS] [SEP]

## 6\. [CLS] [SEP] privacy policy changes & data breach

when we update our privacy policy, we will notify our users within 72 hours
period so that they have time to decide whether to continue using our services
or not. [CLS] [SEP]

in event of a data breach whereby user data or organisation data is leaked, we
will notify users within 72 hours of the breach occuring so they have time to
change emails, passwords and allow users to download their data should they
wish to use another service. [CLS] [SEP]

## 7\. [CLS] [SEP] making a complaint & seeking further information

if you are unhappy with the way your personal information is being handled,
you have the right to make a complaint to the information commissioner's
office, who enforces and oversees the data protection act 1998, which will
investigate your complaint. [CLS] [SEP]

for further information about the personal information collected and its' use,
if you have concerns about the accuracy of personal information, or wish to
excercise your rights under the data protection act 1998, you should [contact
us](contact.php) or the information commissioner's office help line via: 01625
545745. [CLS] [SEP]
