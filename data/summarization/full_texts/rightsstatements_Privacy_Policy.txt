# privacy policy

the rightsstatements.org consortium collects, manages and uses your data so
that we can run our services and share relevant content with you. [CLS] [SEP] this policy
describes how we collect and use your data when you use our services through
the website or in person, and the measures we take to ensure your data remains
secure. [CLS] [SEP] europeana foundation runs these services on behalf of the
rightsstatements.org consortium and takes responsibility for these data. [CLS] [SEP]

## keeping your data secure

your privacy is important to us. [CLS] [SEP] we take all steps necessary to ensure that
your data is stored securely and used only in accordance with the preferences
you have requested. [CLS] [SEP] we will not share your data unless you have given us
permission to share it. [CLS] [SEP] we do not and will not sell your data. [CLS] [SEP] where possible,
we use services that are hosted within the european union. [CLS] [SEP]

## collecting your data

### we collect information through cookies

when you use a rightsstatements.org website, we use
[cookies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/http_cookie) to collect information
about your settings and preferences so that these can be used each time you
visit one of our websites. [CLS] [SEP] we also collect information that is not specific to
your identity such as information collected automatically through cookies,
e.g. [CLS] [SEP] your language preferences. [CLS] [SEP]

## managing your data

you can manage cookies by checking the settings for each browser you use. [CLS] [SEP]

we do not keep your data for any longer than necessary to fulfil the purposes
for which we collected the data or the purposes for which data are reused. [CLS] [SEP]

you may ask us whether we process data relating to you, and if so, which data
this concerns. [CLS] [SEP] in that case, we can provide you with access to the data
processed by us that relates to you. [CLS] [SEP] if you believe your personal data has
been processed incorrectly or incompletely, you may request that we change or
supplement the data (rectification). [CLS] [SEP]

you can contact us via
[info@rightsstatements.org](mailto:info@rightsstatements.org). [CLS] [SEP]

if you have a complaint concerning the processing of your personal data by the
rightsstatements.org consortium or europeana foundation, please

  1. [CLS] [SEP] contact us via [info@rightsstatements.org](mailto:info@rightsstatements.org);
  2. [CLS] [SEP] contact your local data protection authority (dpa). [CLS] [SEP]

## using your data

there are a number of ways that we may use or share your data in order to
provide our services. [CLS] [SEP] if we intend to use or share your data for purposes
other than those described here, we will inform you when we request your data. [CLS] [SEP]
we will only use your data upon your approval and/or if it is necessary to
perform agreements. [CLS] [SEP]

## what this policy applies to

this privacy policy applies to the use of services provided by the
rightsstatements.org consortium. [CLS] [SEP] it is in accordance with the general data
protection regulation of the european union. [CLS] [SEP]
