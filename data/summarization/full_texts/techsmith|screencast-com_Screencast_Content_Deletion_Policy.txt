#  techsmith screencast.com content deletion policy faq

last updated:

    october 05, 2018 12:59

starting november 2nd, 2018, techsmith will begin deleting content that has
not been viewed in the past year from free screencast accounts. [CLS] [SEP] this content
deletion will occur over a long period of time so it is possible you may not
see the content deleted right away. [CLS] [SEP] to learn more about [techsmith
screencast.com content deletion policy](https://www.techsmith.com/screencast-
content-deletion.html) and your content, see the following topics:

  * [will my screencast.com content be deleted?](https://support.techsmith.com/hc/en-us/articles/360002528632)
  * [how do i determine the last viewed date?](https://support.techsmith.com/hc/en-us/articles/360003032872)
  * [how do i export my screencast.com content?](https://support.techsmith.com/hc/en-us/articles/360002543311)
  * [benefits of screencast.com pro vs. [CLS] [SEP] free](https://support.techsmith.com/hc/en-us/articles/227483488)
