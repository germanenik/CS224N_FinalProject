# privacy policy

we value your privacy and as web users ourselves know that you want the least
invasive user experience possible. [CLS] [SEP] we collect, use, and share the least amount
of information possible for our website services to operate. [CLS] [SEP]

this side is the actual privacy policy. [CLS] [SEP] it is legally binding. [CLS] [SEP]

this side is a summary. [CLS] [SEP] it is not legally binding. [CLS] [SEP]

### data we collect from you

we collect your personal information when you submit it to us via a webform or
send us an email. [CLS] [SEP]

we also collect personal transaction information when you are logged into your
account and use services on our site, make purchases, or sell items in our
marketplace. [CLS] [SEP]

### summary,

if you give us your personal info we will have it. [CLS] [SEP] if you have an account we
will keep track of what you buy and sell on the website. [CLS] [SEP]

### how we use your personal information

we use your personal information to contact you about your account and
transactions you make on the site. [CLS] [SEP]

we may provide your personal information to service providers to facilitate
services on the site including but not limited to email delivery and payment
processing. [CLS] [SEP]

### summary,

we will only use your personal information to contact you about your account. [CLS] [SEP]

### sharing data with third parties

when you enter into a transaction on our marketplace, you give us permission
to share your personal information with the other party to the transaction. [CLS] [SEP] we
will only share information needed for order fulfilment. [CLS] [SEP] we will never share
payment information, passwords, or phone numbers. [CLS] [SEP]

we do not sell, rent, or give away any of your personal information to any
other third parties. [CLS] [SEP]

we will only share your personal and transaction information with law
enforcement personnel when presented with a valid court order. [CLS] [SEP]

### summary,

we share your information with people on the marketplace only when needed to
complete a transaction. [CLS] [SEP] we don't share it with anyone else unless the law
requires it. [CLS] [SEP]

### information retention

we retain personal and transaction information indefinitely. [CLS] [SEP] you can request
us to remove your personal information. [CLS] [SEP] to preserve integrity of our database,
we do not remove transaction informaton. [CLS] [SEP] if required by law, we will erase
account information. [CLS] [SEP] you agree that all information submitted to us is the
property of pricecharting and can be used by us in accordance with this
privacy policy. [CLS] [SEP]

### summary,

we can keep and use your information until you ask us to remove it. [CLS] [SEP]

### cookies

when you visit pricecharting we might sent cookies to your computer. [CLS] [SEP] we use
this information to remember user preferences, logged in status, and for some
website features. [CLS] [SEP]

some third party services deliver cookies as well. [CLS] [SEP] we use these to know
traffic numbers and what features customers use. [CLS] [SEP]

at any time you can block cookies in your browser. [CLS] [SEP] some features will not work
correctly with our cookies turned off. [CLS] [SEP] there are no negative consequences to
disabling third party cookies. [CLS] [SEP]

### summary,

we use some cookies for website features like logging in. [CLS] [SEP] some features won't
work if you block our cookies. [CLS] [SEP]
