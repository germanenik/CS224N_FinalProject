sometimes we use data about your interests or demographics that some of our global third parties have collected from you online to add you to these groups, such as comscore and neilsen .<q>_ personal data shared by event partners _ when you register or book a ticket for a guardian event organised by an event partner, your registration data may be shared with us by the event partner .<q>
