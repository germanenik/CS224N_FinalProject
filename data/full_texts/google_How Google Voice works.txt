# how google voice works

google voice stores, processes and maintains your call history (including
calling party phone number, called party phone number, date, time and duration
of call), voicemail greeting(s), voicemail messages, short message service
(sms) messages, recorded conversations, and other data related to your account
in order to provide the service to you.

you may delete your call history, voicemail greeting(s), voicemail messages
(both audio and/or transcriptions), short message service (sms) messages, and
recorded conversations through your google voice account, although your call
history for billable calls may remain visible on your account. some
information may be retained on our active servers temporarily for billing or
other business purposes, and residual copies may remain in our backup systems.
anonymized copies of call record information, with no personally identifiable
information, will be retained in our systems to meet our reporting and
auditing requirements.
