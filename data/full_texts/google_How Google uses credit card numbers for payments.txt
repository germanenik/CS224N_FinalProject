# how google uses credit card numbers for payments

google uses the credit card and debit card numbers you provide to facilitate
payments for the online or offline purchases you make, including google play
and google pay transactions, and for fraud mitigation purposes. the [google
payments privacy
notice](https://payments.google.com/legaldocument?family=0.privacynotice&hl=en)
provides detailed information about how we use your payment and account
information, including the information we collect and how we share it. we only
share personal information with third parties in the circumstances described
in the google payments privacy notice. the credit card and debit card numbers
you provide to google are encrypted and stored on secure servers in a secure
location.
