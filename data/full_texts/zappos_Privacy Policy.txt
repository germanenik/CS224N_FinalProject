# privacy notice

 **last updated: january 15, 2020**

zappos.com llc knows that you care how information about you is used and
shared, and we appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and
sensibly. this notice describes our privacy policy. **by visiting zappos.com,
you are accepting the practices described in this privacy notice.**

## what personal information about customers does zappos.com gather?

the information we collect and learn from customers helps us personalize and
continually improve your zappos experience. one of our goals in collecting
personal information from you is to provide an efficient, meaningful, and
customized experience. here are the general categories of information we
gather:

• **information you give us:** we receive and store information you enter on
our website, or give us in any other way. examples include, but are not
limited to, information when you search, browse, buy, post reviews,
participate in a sweepstakes or questionnaire, or communicate with customer
service. we use the information that you provide for purposes such as
responding to your requests, processing your order, customizing future
shopping experiences, responding to you, or sending you marketing and
advertising communications. if we encounter a problem when processing your
order, your personal information may be used to contact you. please note that
before accessing certain parts of zappos.com or completing an order, you must
create an account with us, through which you must provide certain personal
information.  
• **automatic information:** we receive and store certain types of information
automatically whenever you interact with us. for example, like many websites,
we use "cookies" or other unique identifiers and we obtain certain types of
information when your web browser accesses zappos.com or advertisements and
other content served by, or on behalf of, zappos.com on other websites. other
examples include, but are not limited to, log files, internet protocol (ip)
address used to connect your computer to the internet, and internet browser
type.  
• **mobile:** when you download or use apps created by zappos, we may receive
information about your location and your mobile device, including a unique
identifier for your device. we may use this information to provide you with
location-based services, such as advertising, search results, and other
personalized content. most mobile devices allow you to turn off location
services.  
• **e-mail communications:** to help us make emails more useful and
interesting, we often receive a confirmation when you open email from
zappos.com, if your computer supports such capabilities. if you do not want to
receive email from us (unrelated to transactions and/or your account), please
adjust your email subscription settings in your zappos account or
[contact](https://www.zappos.com/c/contact-us) our customer service team.  
• **information from other sources:** we might receive information about you
from other sources and add it to our account information. examples include,
but are not limited to, updated delivery and address information from our
carriers and information from credit bureaus, which we use to help prevent and
detect fraud.

## what about cookies?

• web beacons (also known as action tags or single-pixel gifs), and other
unique identifiers (collectively, "cookies") are small pieces of information
that are stored by your internet browser on your computer’s hard drive. like
many websites, zappos uses cookies to speed your navigation of zappos.com,
enable you to select products, place them in an online shopping cart, and to
purchase those products, along with allowing us to maintain a record of your
browsing activity on our websites. cookies also allow our systems to recognize
your device and to provide features such as recently viewed items, brand
recommendations, and personalized zappos advertisements on other websites. our
cookies do not and cannot infiltrate your hard drive to gather your
confidential information. our cookies are not "spyware."  
• most internet browsers are initially set to accept cookies. because cookies
allow you to take advantage of some of zappos.com's essential features, we
recommend that you leave them turned on. you can, however, set your browser to
refuse cookies from websites or to remove cookies from your hard drive, but if
you do, you may not be able to access or use portions of zappos.com. for
instance, if you block or otherwise reject our cookies, you will not be able
to add items to your shopping cart, proceed to checkout, or use any zappos.com
products or services that require you to sign in.

## does zappos.com share the information it receives?

information about our customers is an important part of our business, and we
are not in the business of selling, renting or trading it to others. we share
customer information only as described in this privacy notice and with
entities within the zappos family of companies. the "zappos family of
companies" means zappos.com llc and its subsidiaries.  
• **third-party service providers:** we may employ other companies and
individuals to perform functions on our behalf. examples include, but are not
limited to, fulfilling orders, delivering packages, sending postal mail and
email, removing repetitive information from customer lists, analyzing data,
providing marketing assistance, providing search results and links (including
paid listings and links), processing credit card payments, providing customer
service, storing data, and serving ads on our behalf. they have access to
personal information needed to perform their functions, but may not use it for
other purposes.  
• **promotional offers:** sometimes we send offers to selected groups of
zappos.com customers in collaboration with other businesses. when we do this,
we do not allow that business to access your information. you may have the
opportunity to separately share information with those other businesses, and
if you do, that information would be governed by such company’s privacy
policy. you may adjust your [email subscription
settings](https://www.zappos.com/subscriptions) in your zappos.com account or
by contacting our [customer service team](https://www.zappos.com/c/contact-
us).  
• **business transfers:** as we continue to develop our business, we might
sell or buy stores, companies, or business units. in such transactions,
customer information generally is one of the transferred business assets but
remains subject to the promises made in any pre-existing privacy notice
(unless, of course, the customer consents otherwise). also, in the unlikely
event that zappos, or substantially all of its assets are acquired, customer
information will of course be one of the transferred assets.  
• **protection of zappos.com and others:** we release account and other
personal information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the
law; enforce or apply our [conditions of use](https://www.zappos.com/terms-of-
use) and other agreements; or protect the rights, property, or safety of
zappos.com, our customers, or others. this includes exchanging information
with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk
reduction. obviously, however, this does not include selling, renting,
sharing, or otherwise disclosing personally identifiable information from
customers for commercial purposes in violation of the commitments set forth in
this privacy notice.  
• **with your consent:** other than as set out above, we may seek your consent
for us to share your information with additional third parties, and you will
have the opportunity in your discretion to choose not to share your
information.

## how secure is information about me?

• we work to protect the security of your information by utilizing encryption
methods during the transmission or storage of information you input through
our websites and mobile applications. one hundred percent complete security
does not presently exist anywhere online or offline, and we are unable to
provide specific guarantees regarding complete security of your personal
information; however, we have a team of security professionals that work to
safeguard your information.  
• it is important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your
password and to your computer. be sure to sign off and close your browser when
finished using a shared computer. only use trusted internet networks,
including wi-fi, and do no share your login credentials with unauthorized
parties.

## what about third-party advertisers and links to other websites?

zappos may utilize interested-based advertising (also known as personalized
ads or targeted ads) on both zappos-owned and operated websites and
unaffiliated sites, and such ads may be displayed to you by these third party
service providers based on information from activities such as purchasing
products online (including from zappos.com) or visiting third-party websites.
for more information, please read our [interest-based ads
policy](https://www.zappos.com/c/interest-based-ads-policy).

## which information can i access?

zappos.com gives you access to information about your account and your
interactions with zappos.com for the limited purpose of viewing and, in
certain cases, updating that information. examples include account order
history shipping address(es), favorites, contact information, etc. this
information may change as our website evolves.

## what choices do i have?

• you can always choose not to provide certain information; however, it might
be needed to take advantage of certain zappos.com features such as your
account profile, favorites and customer reviews. before ordering products from
zappos.com, you must create a valid account.  
• if you do not want to receive marketing emails from us, please adjust your
[email subscription preferences](https://www.zappos.com/subscriptions) in your
account or click the "unsubscribe" link in the email. if you do not want to
receive physical marketing mail from us, please reach out to our [customer
service team](https://www.zappos.com/c/contact-us). (regardless if you receive
our [conditions of use](https://www.zappos.com/terms-of-use) and other legal
notices from us, such as this privacy notice, those notices will still govern
your use of zappos.com, and it is your responsibility to review them for
changes.)  
• upon your request, we will (a) correct or update your personal information;
(b) stop sending non-transactional emails to your email address and/or
physical mail to your address; and/or (c) disable your account to prevent any
future purchases through that account. you can make these requests by
contacting our [customer service team](https://www.zappos.com/c/contact-us).  
• for state-specific privacy choices, please [click
here](https://www.zappos.com/c/state-privacy).

## are children allowed to use zappos.com?

zappos.com does not sell products for purchase by children. we sell children's
products for purchase by adults. if you are under 18, you may use zappos.com
only with the involvement and permission of a parent or guardian. we do not
knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13
